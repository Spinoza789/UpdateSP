What should the dashboard lead with?

The top of the dashboard sets the tone. Which of these feels right for how you actually use the admin?

A

Revenue first

Big money numbers front and centre — today, this week, this month. Orders and members are secondary. Good if you open the admin to check how sales are going.

$12,450 today $67k this month Revenue chart
B

Operations first

What needs your attention right now — orders to process, pending payments, open tickets, low stock alerts. Revenue is there but in the background. Good if you use the admin to action things daily.

14 orders pending 3 open tickets 2 low stock
C

Hybrid — stats row + action feed

A tight row of 4–6 KPI chips (revenue, orders, members, active GBs) at the top, then the rest of the page is a live action feed — recent orders, alerts, tickets — prioritised by urgency. Best of both.
