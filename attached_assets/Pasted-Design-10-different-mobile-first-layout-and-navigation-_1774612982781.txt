Requirements:

Preserve all existing content and labels; only reorganise structure, hierarchy, and layout.

The calculators, lab reports, and safety tools should be grouped into a clearly labelled “Tools & Lab” area, accessible via secondary navigation (tabs, section, or separate screen) instead of being scattered through the main purchase flows.

Avoid information overload: use clear grouping, white space, and progressive disclosure so users first choose between Group Buys, Single Vials, and Tools, then see details within that area.
